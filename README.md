Impact of US-Iran Conflict on Global Oil Prices:

Project Overview
This project analyzes how geopolitical conflicts affect global energy markets.
Using Power BI dashboards, the project visualizes the relationship between war events, crude oil price volatility, petrol price increases, and economic vulnerability across countries.

1)Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- CSV datasets

2) Datasets
- Crude Oil Daily Price Dataset
- Petrol Price Comparison Dataset
- War Timeline Dataset
- Country Economic Impact Dataset

3)Dashboards:
i. Oil Market Volatility
Shows crude oil price trends and war event impact.

ii. Petrol Price Impact
Shows petrol price increases across countries.

iii. Economic Impact Analysis
Shows vulnerability of countries affected by rising oil prices.

Key Insights
- Oil prices increased during conflict escalation.
- Countries with high oil import dependency faced larger petrol price increases.
- Economic indicators such as GDP impact and inflation risk were correlated with oil price changes.
